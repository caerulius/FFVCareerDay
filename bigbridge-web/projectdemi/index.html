What is Project Demi ?

A Final Fantasy 5 Quality of Life romhack that breathes new life into a classic game.

Enhanced

Toggle random encounters. Optional patches for increased EXP and ABP from battles. Walking & dashing speed increased.

Streamlined

Long cutscenes sped up or cut entirely, gamefeel and charm retained.

Welcoming

A new menu screen will always point you in the right direction if you get lost.
